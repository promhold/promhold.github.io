Company Overview

Built on Purpose.
Driven by Promise.

Promise and Provision Holdings LLC is a Wyoming-registered holding company established to identify, develop, and support purpose-driven subsidiary businesses operating across Texas and beyond.

Our holding company structure provides strategic oversight, financial stewardship, and organizational clarity across our portfolio — ensuring each subsidiary has the foundation it needs to serve its community effectively and grow sustainably.

We believe that business, done right, is one of the most powerful tools for creating lasting positive change — for workers, families, and communities alike.

Holding Company with Operating Subsidiaries

Promise and Provision Holdings LLC was founded on the belief that purposeful business creation — grounded in community need and guided by sound values — is one of the most effective paths to lasting positive impact.

As a holding company, our role is to provide the strategic oversight, organizational structure, and financial management that allow our subsidiary businesses to thrive. We handle the foundational work so our operating businesses can focus on what they do best: serving people well.

Our Approach

We believe in starting lean and growing smart. Rather than taking on unnecessary debt or overhead, we build businesses from the ground up — proving the model first, then scaling responsibly using profits reinvested from operations.

Our businesses are designed to fill gaps that others overlook: serving working-class communities, creating employment, and doing so in a way that is environmentally and financially sustainable for the long term.

Wyoming Formation

Promise and Provision Holdings LLC is proudly registered in the state of Wyoming, which offers a business-friendly legal environment, strong liability protections, and a flexible LLC structure well-suited for holding company operations. Our primary business activities and community investments are centered in the Houston, Texas metropolitan area.

Sustainability

Reducing Waste.
Creating Value.

A core principle of Promise and Provision Holdings LLC is our commitment to reducing waste by maximizing the value of what already exists. Across our subsidiaries, we deliberately choose pre-owned assets over new ones wherever possible — extending useful lifespans, reducing manufacturing demand, and keeping resources in productive use.

We believe that sustainability is not just an environmental responsibility — it is smart business. By giving existing assets a purposeful second life, we lower costs, serve communities more affordably, and demonstrate that doing right by the planet and doing right by people are not competing goals.

This approach guides our acquisition strategies, our operational decisions, and the values we instill in every business operating under our umbrella.
