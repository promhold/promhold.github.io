Philosophy
Purpose Before Profit

Promise and Provision Holdings LLC was founded on the belief that purposeful business creation — grounded in community need and guided by sound values — is one of the most effective paths to lasting positive impact.

As a holding company, our role is to provide the strategic oversight, organizational structure, and financial management that allow our subsidiary businesses to thrive. We handle the foundational work so our operating businesses can focus on what they do best: serving people well.

Our Approach

We believe in starting lean and growing smart. Rather than taking on unnecessary debt or overhead, we build businesses from the ground up — proving the model first, then scaling responsibly using profits reinvested from operations.

Our businesses are designed to fill gaps that others overlook: serving working-class communities, creating employment, and doing so in a way that is financially and environmentally sustainable for the long term.

Our Commitment

Every decision made under the Promise and Provision Holdings umbrella is filtered through a simple question: does this serve people well? That commitment to people — workers, families, and communities — is the foundation everything else is built on.

Sustainability

Reducing Waste.
Creating Value.

A core principle of Promise and Provision Holdings LLC is our commitment to reducing waste by maximizing the value of what already exists. Across our subsidiaries, we deliberately choose pre-owned assets over new ones wherever possible — extending useful lifespans, reducing manufacturing demand, and keeping resources in productive use.

We believe sustainability is not just an environmental responsibility — it is smart business. By giving existing assets a purposeful second life, we lower costs, serve communities more affordably, and demonstrate that doing right by the planet and doing right by people are not competing goals.

This approach guides our acquisition strategies, our operational decisions, and the values we instill in every business under our umbrella.

Pre-Owned First PolicyPrioritizing existing assets over new manufacturing across all subsidiary operations.
